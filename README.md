# Wand Forge
The Loop
---- Assign Wands ----
 You start with a pool of elemental wands
 and a queue of mages waiting for equipment. 
 Click a wand to select it, then click Assign 
 on a mage to equip them. A mage's stats (HP, MP, Power) 
 are their base values plus whatever the wand adds
 — so better wands mean tankier, harder-hitting mages. 
 You can also Deny a mage to remove them from the queue
 When you're ready hit "Send to battle"

 --- Battle ---
 Combat is automatic. 
 Your mages and the enemies trade blows
 each tick until one side is wiped out. 
 Levels have multiple waves clear a wave 
 and your surviving mages heal 20% HP before 
 the next one begins

 Tips:
 Wand stats vary wildly,
 Affinity matters. Each mage has an elemental affinity shown on their card.
 Don't over-assign.
 Enemies scale hard
